Welcome to Kick The Buddy!

Voodoodoll simulator. Explode, destroy, fire, shoot, freeze, exert Gods superpower and let it go. You now have almost endless Arsenal. Use flying knives, submachine guns, rocket launchers, laser guns, flame throwers and even tanks to destroy it. Release your anger and dispel your anger. Move your hand of God, the puppets will not move and let you play as you hang. If you are tired you can release your hands and let the weapon shoot automatically.
